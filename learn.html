Sukha Chicken recepie

Ingredients

  • Refined oil 1 tsp
  • Ginger and garlic paste 2tsp
  • Chicken 400gm
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Heeng (Compounded Asafoetida) - a pinch
  • Turmeric - 1/3 tsp
  • Red chilli Powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander seed powder (Dhania) - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala - 2 tsp
  • Black Pepper - a pinch
  • Cumin Powder - 1/2 tsp
